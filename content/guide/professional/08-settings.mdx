## Settings & account

The **Settings** section is where you manage your profile, your museum details, your subscription, billing history, and document storage. Click **Settings** at the bottom of the left sidebar to reach it.

## Your profile

<Steps title="How do I update my name or email address?">
  <Step number={1}>Click **Settings** in the sidebar. The Settings page opens to your profile by default.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Update the **Name** or **Email** field as needed.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>If you change your email address, a verification email is sent to the new address. You must click the link in that email before the change takes effect.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Click **Save changes**.</Step>
</Steps>

<Steps title="How do I change my password?">
  <Step number={1}>Go to **Settings → Security** and click **Change password**.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Enter your current password to confirm your identity, then enter and confirm your new password.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click **Update password**. Any other active sessions will be signed out automatically.</Step>
</Steps>

## Plan and billing

<Steps title="How do I see my current plan and usage?">
  <Step number={1}>Go to **Settings → Plan**.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Your current plan, monthly cost, and next billing date are shown at the top of the page.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Below that, usage bars show how much of your object capacity, staff accounts, and document storage you've used.</Step>
</Steps>

<Steps title="How do I upgrade from Professional to Institution?">
  <Step number={1}>Go to **Settings → Plan** and click **Upgrade**.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Select **Institution** from the plan options. The page will show the price difference and a note that you'll be billed pro-rata for the remainder of the current billing period.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Confirm the upgrade. Your account upgrades immediately. Increased limits and any new features are available right away.</Step>
</Steps>

<Steps title="How do I enquire about an Enterprise plan?">
  <Step number={1}>Go to **Settings → Plan** and click **Upgrade**.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Select **Enterprise**. Instead of a payment form, you'll see a prompt to contact the Vitrine team.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click **Contact us about Enterprise** to open an email to hello@vitrine.app. Describe your institution's size, requirements, and any questions you have. We'll get back to you with a custom quote.</Step>
</Steps>

<Steps title="How do I download a copy of an invoice?">
  <Step number={1}>Go to **Settings → Billing**.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Your full billing history is listed in reverse chronological order, with the date, amount, and plan for each charge.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click the **Download** link next to any invoice to download it as a PDF.</Step>
</Steps>

## Document storage

<Steps title="How do I check my document storage usage?">
  <Step number={1}>Go to **Settings → Plan**.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>The **Document storage** row in the usage section shows how much you've used and your plan's total allowance (1 GB on Professional, 10 GB on Institution, unlimited on Enterprise).</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  If you're approaching your storage limit on Professional (1 GB), consider whether any older or superseded documents can be removed. Open an object's Documents tab, find the file, and click the delete icon next to it. Alternatively, upgrade to Institution for 10 GB of storage.
</Callout>

## Museum details

<Steps title="How do I update my museum name?">
  <Step number={1}>Go to **Settings → Museum**.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Update the **Museum name** field. This name appears on your public website, in event booking confirmations, and in all other automated emails sent on your behalf.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click **Save changes**.</Step>
</Steps>

## Cancelling your subscription

<Steps title="How do I cancel my Professional or Institution subscription?">
  <Step number={1}>Go to **Settings → Plan** and click **Cancel subscription**.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Read the confirmation dialogue carefully; it explains what changes when the plan expires.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Confirm the cancellation. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. When it expires, your account reverts to the free Community plan.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Your objects, images, and site settings are all preserved. You lose access to analytics, compliance tools, ticketing, additional staff accounts, and document storage, but nothing is deleted.</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  If you have staff members with Curator, Registrar, or Volunteer roles, they will lose access to the dashboard when your plan reverts to Community (which only supports a single account). They won't be deleted, but they'll be unable to sign in until you re-upgrade to a paid plan.
</Callout>
